## Episode 1: "What Does It Mean to Be a Cyborg?"
**Type:** Internal Discussion (Launch Episode)
**Host:** Kiyasha
**Guest:** Justin

### Episode Overview
Launch episode establishing the core philosophy and setting the tone for the podcast. Define what "cyborg" means in this context and why it matters.

### Key Talking Points
1. **The Cyborg Definition**
   - Not science fiction implants
   - Someone who effectively interacts with AI to increase capabilities
   - Andy Clark's Extended Mind Hypothesis
   - "Natural-Born Cyborgs" concept

2. **Why This Matters Now**
   - Awareness asymmetry as the new competitive edge
   - The gap between what AI can do and what people know
   - Widespread anxiety about future relevance

3. **The Transparent Equipment Vision**
   - Technology becoming invisible
   - The musician/instrument analogy
   - AI as thinking partner, not just tool

4. **The Iron Man Metaphor**
   - Human + AI + Tools = Functional Whole
   - Assessing the integrated system

5. **What This Podcast Will Explore**
   - Future of work
   - Future of education
   - Human-AI collaboration
   - Practical skills and habits

### Quotable Moment Targets
- Definition of cyborg
- Awareness asymmetry concept
- Transparent equipment vision

### CTA
"If you're curious about what's possible when humans and AI work together, subscribe. We're just getting started."

## Episode 2: "The 7 Habits of Highly Effective Cyborgs"
**Type:** Internal Discussion
**Host:** Kiyasha
**Guest:** Justin

### Episode Overview
Deep dive into the 7 habits framework - what they are, why they matter, and how they transform work.

### Key Talking Points
1. **Why Habits, Not Training**
   - Behavior change vs knowledge transfer
   - Making AI use automatic
   - In-flow-of-work principle

2. **The 7 Habits Overview**
   - Explain It
   - Plan It
   - Critique It
   - Guide Me
   - Imagine It
   - What If
   - Improve It

3. **How They Work Together**
   - Not linear - use as needed
   - Combinations for complex tasks
   - Building fluency over time

4. **Damascus Moments**
   - What they are
   - Examples from real users
   - How habits create them

5. **Getting Started**
   - Pick one habit
   - Use it daily for a week
   - Build from there

### Quotable Moment Targets
- "Seven Habits of Highly Effective Cyborgs" reference
- Damascus moment definition
- Behavior change vs learning distinction

### CTA
"Pick one habit. Use it every day this week. Notice what changes. Then come back and tell us about it."

## Production Notes

### Recording Setup
- Remote: Riverside.fm or similar
- Local: Quality microphones in quiet space
- Always have backup recording

### Episode Template
```
[0:00-2:00]   Intro & Episode Preview
[2:00-5:00]   Guest/Topic Introduction
[5:00-25:00]  Main Content Part 1
[25:00-27:00] Mid-break (optional)
[27:00-40:00] Main Content Part 2
[40:00-45:00] Key Takeaways & Close
```

- [ ] Add chapter markers
- [ ] Create transcript

- [ ] Design episode art
- [ ] Schedule publication
