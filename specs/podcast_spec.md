# Podcast Production Specification

## Purpose

Launch a podcast that:
1. Establishes thought leadership in human-AI collaboration space
2. Harvests expert perspectives on future of work/education
3. Creates additional content for the ecosystem pipeline
4. Builds audience and email list
5. Provides audio content for different consumption preferences

## Podcast Concept

### Title Options
- "The Cyborg Perspective"
- "Transparent Equipment"
- "Human + AI"
- "The Cyborg Skills Podcast"

### Tagline
"Conversations about working, thinking, and creating in an AI-enhanced world"

### Format Options

#### Option A: Interview Format (Recommended for Launch)
- Host + external guest
- 30-45 minutes per episode
- Expert perspectives on future of work, education, AI
- Builds network and credibility through guests

#### Option B: Internal Discussions
- Team members discussing concepts
- Can use existing Scream Session content
- More control, less coordination
- Good for supplementary content

#### Option C: Hybrid/Synthetic
- Mix of AI-generated segments and live content
- Use 11 Labs for some narration
- Novel approach, matches brand
- Risk: Authenticity questions

### Recommended Approach
Start with **Option A (Interviews)** as primary format, supplement with **Option B (Internal)** episodes when needed.

## Target Audience

- Professionals concerned about AI's impact on their work
- L&D leaders exploring AI training
- Business leaders making AI strategy decisions
- Educators rethinking curriculum
- Anyone curious about human-AI collaboration

## Episode Structure

### Standard Episode (45 min)
```
[0:00-2:00]   Intro & Episode Preview
[2:00-5:00]   Guest Introduction
[5:00-25:00]  Main Conversation - Part 1
[25:00-27:00] Mid-roll (optional sponsorship/CTA)
[27:00-42:00] Main Conversation - Part 2
[42:00-45:00] Key Takeaways & Close
```

### Episode Sections

**Intro (2 min)**
- Cold open with compelling quote from episode
- Theme music
- Host welcome
- Episode preview

**Guest Introduction (3 min)**
- Guest background
- Why they're relevant to topic
- What we'll explore

**Main Conversation (35 min)**
- Prepared questions but conversational
- Follow interesting threads
- Challenge assumptions
- Seek concrete examples and stories

**Close (3 min)**
- Guest's key message
- Where to find guest
- CTA for listeners
- Next episode preview

## Production Workflow

### Pre-Production (1 week before)
1. Confirm guest and schedule
2. Research guest background
3. Prepare 10-12 questions
4. Send guest prep document
5. Test recording setup

### Recording Day
1. Pre-recording chat (5 min)
2. Sound check
3. Record episode (45-60 min)
4. Post-recording debrief
5. Note key timestamps

### Post-Production (3-5 days)
1. Edit recording (remove ums, long pauses)
2. Add intro/outro music
3. Insert mid-roll if applicable
4. Create transcript
5. Write show notes
6. Design episode artwork
7. Export final audio

### Publication
1. Upload to hosting platform
2. Write episode description
3. Create social assets
4. Schedule email to subscribers
5. Publish and promote

## Technical Setup

### Recording Options

#### Remote Recording (Primary)
- **Platform**: Riverside.fm, Zencastr, or SquadCast
- **Backup**: Zoom with local recording
- **Requirements**: Guest has decent mic and quiet space

#### In-Person Recording (Optional)
- **Equipment**: Quality microphones, portable recorder
- **Location**: Quiet meeting room
- **For**: Local guests or special episodes

### Audio Specifications
- **Format**: WAV or high-quality MP3
- **Sample rate**: 44.1kHz or 48kHz
- **Bit depth**: 16-bit minimum
- **Loudness**: -16 LUFS (podcast standard)

### Editing Software
- Descript (AI-assisted editing)
- Adobe Audition
- Audacity (free option)
- GarageBand (Mac)

### Hosting Platform
- Options: Spotify for Podcasters, Buzzsprout, Transistor, Anchor
- Requirements: RSS feed, analytics, distribution to major platforms
- Cost: $12-20/month for quality platform

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Guest no-shows | Episode gaps | Bank episodes, have backup plan |
| Audio quality issues | Poor listener experience | Pre-call tech check, backup recording |
| Low initial downloads | Discouragement | Set realistic expectations, focus on content quality |
| Host burnout | Inconsistent releases | Sustainable cadence, batch recording |
| Competitive market | Discovery challenges | Niche focus, guest audience leverage |
